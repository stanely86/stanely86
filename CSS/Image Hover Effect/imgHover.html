AI painting refers to the use of artificial intelligence algorithms to create digital artworks autonomously or in collaboration with human artists. Through machine learning techniques, AI systems analyze patterns, styles, and compositions, generating diverse and innovative visual creations across various mediums, revolutionizing the traditional art-making process.
